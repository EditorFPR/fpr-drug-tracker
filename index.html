Methodology

Every entry must carry at least one named source before it is treated as verified. Entries without a confirmed source carry a visible "Source pending" badge and are excluded from aggregate claims in FPR reporting.

Quantity figures use the most authoritative available figure: forensic/court-confirmed preferred over police press release. Where figures differ between sources, both are noted. Drug type attribution follows court charges or forensic confirmation, not preliminary field testing alone.

Institutional integrity events (corruption, officer charges, unit dissolutions) are included because they are material to governance analysis — each reflects an official charge, parliamentary statement, or court record. Anchor events (red border) are the defining inflection points in Fiji's trafficking escalation.

This tracker does not constitute legal opinion. Case status reflects the most recent verified information available; outcomes change.
